Common questions

Things brands ask first.

Who exactly receives the push?
Every active NorCal Holistics app user with push notifications enabled — currently 6,000+ verified cannabis customers, primarily in the greater Sacramento delivery zone. These are paying customers, not sign-up lurkers.
Do I write the push copy?
No — we do. Our marketing team writes the promo around your brand, our voice, our app's tone. You'll see the draft before it goes out and can request tweaks. This keeps the push consistent with what our customers expect from us, which means better open and tap rates for your feature.
What time does the push go out?
Default: 7:00 PM PT, our highest-engagement window. We can shift ±2 hours if you have a specific drop time — just leave a note in the booking form.
What if I want to provide promo items?
Totally optional — and a great way to increase pull. If you toggle on "provide promo items" in the booking form, we'll work with you on samples, discount codes, free-add-on items, or swag bundles to make the deal even sharper. Not required. The promo runs either way.
What happens if my slot sells between submission and payment?
Slots confirm in order of payment received. If your chosen date sells before your $99 payment clears, we refund you in full and offer the next available date of your choice. Submitting the form doesn't hold the slot — completed payment does.
Can I book multiple days?
Yes. Submit the form once per date you want — each is $99. Want to lock in a recurring weekly or monthly placement? Email seth@norcalholistics.org for bundle pricing.
What's your cancellation policy?
Full refund if canceled more than 48 hours before send time. Inside 48 hours: 50% refund or free reschedule to any available date within 60 days. After send: no refunds (the work is done).
Any content restrictions?
No competing delivery services. No content that violates state cannabis advertising law (Prop 64). Otherwise we keep approval friction low — we'll flag anything that looks risky before send.
